Moving house in London - what you need to know

London house moves have specific practical challenges our team manages every day. Controlled Parking Zones cover the majority of London streets - a removal vehicle cannot park legally without a formal parking suspension from the relevant borough.

🚗
Parking suspensions - we handle this for you Relokates manages parking suspension applications for all London moves as part of our standard service. We identify the requirement, apply to the correct borough and confirm the suspension is in place before moving day. You do not need to manage this process yourself.

Properties in London's older housing stock - Victorian and Edwardian terraces throughout South East, East and North London - often have narrow hallways, steep stairs and limited turning space. Our crew carries out a pre-move assessment of these properties to select the right vehicle size and plan the most efficient loading sequence. We also carry specialist equipment including stair-climbing trolleys, furniture sliders and wardrobe boxes for London flat and apartment moves.

Moving house in Essex

Essex is one of our most active regions. The county's mix of established towns - Chelmsford, Colchester, Brentwood, Harlow, Basildon - and smaller commuter villages creates a consistently busy removals market with strong demand for moves both within Essex and between Essex and London. The A12, A13 and M25 provide good road access across the county, but peak hour traffic on the A12 corridor particularly requires early morning starts on moving day.

We cover all Essex postcodes from Southend-on-Sea in the south to Saffron Walden in the north, and from the Thames Estuary towns in the west to the coast at Mersea Island and Frinton-on-Sea in the east. Whether you are moving from a Victorian terrace in Chelmsford to a new build in Colchester or from Basildon to a London borough, our Essex team has the local knowledge and the route familiarity to handle your move efficiently.

Moving house in Kent

Kent's diversity as a county creates genuinely varied removal requirements. The historic city centres of Canterbury and Rochester have narrow medieval streets requiring careful vehicle selection. The coastal towns of Whitstable, Herne Bay, Folkestone and Margate attract significant relocation activity from London buyers seeking seaside living. The commuter towns of Sevenoaks, Tonbridge and Tunbridge Wells serve a professional population with frequent moves both within Kent and to and from London.

We cover all Kent postcodes including all Medway towns (Rochester, Chatham, Gillingham, Strood), all Thanet and East Kent towns and all West Kent commuter towns. The M20, M2 and A2 road network means Kent moves are generally well-served by road - but specific access challenges in Canterbury's historic core, on Kent's coastal cliffs and on the North Downs country lanes require local knowledge we have built over years of Kent removals.

Moving house in West Sussex

West Sussex combines the busy coastal towns of Brighton, Worthing, Bognor Regis and Littlehampton with the affluent inland towns of Horsham, Haywards Heath, Burgess Hill and East Grinstead. The county attracts strong relocation demand from London buyers and from families moving within Sussex seeking more space. The A23 and M23 motorway corridor is the main route for London to Sussex moves, with journey times of 45 to 90 minutes depending on origin and destination.

We cover all West Sussex postcodes and carry out a high volume of moves on the Brighton to London and Worthing to Surrey routes. Our West Sussex team has specific experience in coastal property access - many of the most desirable homes in Worthing, Bognor Regis and Chichester are in streets with restricted vehicle access that require advance planning.

Specialist house removal services

Packing services

Our professional packing service takes the most time-consuming and stressful part of moving off your plate entirely. Our packers arrive the day before your move with all materials - double-walled removal boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, wardrobe boxes and specialist covers for mirrors, artwork and televisions - and pack your entire home systematically. Everything is labelled by room and content so unloading at the new property is fast and organised. The packing service is priced separately and added to your fixed-price removal quotation.

Specialist item handling

Grand pianos, upright pianos, large antique furniture, American-style fridge freezers, gym equipment and heavy safes all require equipment and experience beyond standard removal capability. Relokates carries specialist equipment including piano dollies, stair-climbing trolleys and heavy lifting boards. All specialist items must be declared when requesting your quotation so we can plan the appropriate crew size and equipment for your move.

Storage during your house move

A completion gap between your move-out and move-in date is one of the most common challenges in the UK property market. Relokates provides a seamless storage solution - we collect from your current property, store your belongings in our secure facility, and deliver to your new property when it is ready. Storage is priced on a weekly basis and added to your removal quotation. There is no need to arrange separate storage and a separate removal - we handle both as a single, coordinated service.
