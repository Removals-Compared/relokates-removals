Moving house in London — what you need to know

London house moves have specific practical challenges our team manages every day. Controlled Parking Zones cover the majority of London streets — a removal vehicle cannot park legally without a formal parking suspension from the relevant borough.

🚗
Parking suspensions — we handle this for you Relokates manages parking suspension applications for all London moves as part of our standard service. We identify the requirement, apply to the correct borough and confirm the suspension is in place before moving day. You do not need to manage this process yourself.
