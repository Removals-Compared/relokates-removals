House removal services that put you first

Moving house is consistently rated as one of the most stressful life events a person can go through. At Relokates we take as much of that pressure off your shoulders as possible, managing every aspect of the physical move from arriving at your current property to placing the last box in your new home exactly where you want it.

We cover every property type and size, from studio flats and apartments to large detached family homes. Our team arrives uniformed, on time and fully prepared with the right vehicles and equipment for your specific move.

What our house removal service includes

Every Relokates house removal includes the following as standard:

  • Pre-move planning: We discuss your move in detail before moving day, confirming timings, access and any specialist items.
  • Protective wrapping: All furniture, mirrors and fragile items wrapped in blankets, bubble wrap or specialist covers before moving.
  • Careful loading: Our crew is trained in methodical, weight-appropriate loading that maximises stability during transit.
  • Placement at destination: We place every item where you want it. we do not simply stack boxes in the hallway.
  • Debris removal: We take away all protective materials, leaving your new home clean and ready.

Moving to or from London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex

We cover 475 locations across all four regions. Whether you are moving within the same town, from London to the Kent countryside, or from Essex to West Sussex, our team has the vehicles, route knowledge and local experience to manage your move smoothly.
