House removal services that put you first

Moving house is consistently rated as one of the most stressful life events a person can experience. Between managing a property chain, coordinating solicitors and surveyors, organising your finances, and dealing with the logistics of physically moving everything you own, the pressure can be enormous. At Relokates, we take as much of that pressure off your shoulders as possible.

Our house removal service covers every aspect of the physical move — from arriving at your current property with the right vehicles and equipment to placing the last box in your new home exactly where you want it. We do not rush. We do not cut corners. And we do not leave until you are completely satisfied that everything has arrived safely and is in the right place.

We serve over 475 locations across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex. Whether you are moving within the same street in Chelmsford, relocating from a London borough to the Kent countryside, or making a longer distance move from West Sussex to Essex, our team has the experience, vehicles and local knowledge to manage your move from start to finish.

What our house removal service includes

Every Relokates house removal includes the following as standard — there are no extras that should be obvious inclusions but are billed separately:

  • Pre-move planning: We discuss your move in detail before the day, confirming timings, access arrangements, parking requirements and any specific items that need special handling.
  • Protective wrapping: All furniture, mirrors, pictures and fragile items are wrapped in protective blankets, bubble wrap or specialist covers before being moved. Nothing leaves your property unprotected.
  • Careful loading: Our crew is trained in methodical, weight-appropriate loading that maximises stability in the vehicle and minimises movement during transit.
  • Transit to your new property: We drive carefully and responsibly. Long-distance moves within our network are planned with route knowledge and appropriate rest stops for the crew.
  • Careful unloading and placement: At your new property, we place every item where you want it. We do not simply stack boxes in the hallway and leave. We place furniture in the correct rooms and position it as directed.
  • Debris removal: We take away all protective wrapping and packing materials we have used, leaving your new home clean and ready.

How we price our house removal service

Every Relokates house removal quote is fixed price. The figure we provide after discussing your move with you is the amount you will be invoiced. We do not add charges on moving day for stairs, long carries from the van to your front door, parking delays, heavy items such as pianos or safes that you have told us about, or any other foreseeable challenge.

Our pricing is based on several factors that we ask about when you request a quote:

  • The size of your property — number of bedrooms and the volume of belongings you are moving
  • The distance between your current and new property
  • Access conditions at both addresses — parking, lift access, floor level, road restrictions
  • Whether you require packing services in addition to the removal
  • Whether you have specialist items such as a piano, antiques, large artwork or a safe
  • Your preferred move date and any timing constraints on moving day

As a general guide, a one-bedroom flat move within Essex or Kent typically starts from around £350. A three-bedroom house move from London to Kent typically ranges from £650 to £1,000. A five-bedroom family home moving from Surrey to West Sussex would typically range from £1,400 to £2,200. These are indicative figures only — your fixed-price quote will reflect the specific details of your move.

House removals across London

London presents unique challenges for removal companies. Restricted parking zones, congestion charge areas, narrow streets in older residential areas, high-rise blocks with limited lift access, and the sheer density of traffic all make London removals more complex than moves in less urban areas. Relokates has extensive experience managing moves in all 33 London boroughs and understands the specific access and logistics challenges of each area.

For London moves, we apply for parking suspensions on your behalf where needed, plan our arrival time around school run traffic and peak hour congestion, and use appropriately sized vehicles for the streets in your area. A 7.5-tonne luton van that works perfectly in rural Essex is not always the right choice for a terraced street in Hackney or a narrow mews in Kensington.

What size property can you move?

We move every size of residential property — there is no job too small or too large for the Relokates team:

  • Studio and one-bedroom flats: Often moved with a single vehicle and two crew members. The smallest jobs are sometimes the trickiest — ground floor studio flats can be straightforward, while top-floor flats without a lift in a building with narrow staircases require careful planning and execution.
  • Two and three-bedroom properties: The most common move type we carry out. Typically one or two vehicles depending on the volume and the distance.
  • Four and five-bedroom houses: Larger moves that benefit from our pre-move planning service to ensure everything is organised and the move runs efficiently without unnecessary delays.
  • Large six-bedroom and above properties: Our largest house moves often require multiple vehicles and a full day or even two days of moving. We plan these moves carefully in advance and can arrange for packing to be completed the day before if required.
