House removals in Richmond

Relokates provides professional, fully insured house removal services throughout Richmond and all surrounding areas. Whether you are moving within the borough, relocating to another part of London, or moving out of the capital to Essex, Kent or West Sussex, our team manages every aspect of your move from the first call to the final box in your new home.

Richmond upon Thames is consistently ranked among the best places to live in London and regularly appears at the top of UK quality of life surveys. Richmond Park: one of the largest urban parks in the world: anchors the borough's identity. Kew Gardens, the Thames riverside and a string of attractive village centres make the borough uniquely appealing. Property here commands a significant premium and attracts a professional family demographic. The combination of narrow village streets in Richmond and Kew and high property values means removals in this borough require particular care.

Transport and access in Richmond

District line to Richmond and Kew Gardens. Overground through Barnes and Mortlake. National Rail from Twickenham and Richmond. The Thames Path cycling and walking routes supplement public transport.

Understanding the transport geography of Richmond informs how we plan every removal in the borough. We know which streets have permit parking zones that require suspension applications, which areas have loading restrictions during certain hours, which roads have restricted access for larger vehicles, and which routes provide the most efficient access during different times of day. This local knowledge makes a real difference on moving day.

Parking suspensions in Richmond

Most house removals in Richmond require a parking suspension to allow our vehicle to park directly outside your property. Without a suspension, our crew may be forced to park some distance away, turning the move into a long-carry exercise that adds time and difficulty to the job. We apply for parking suspensions on your behalf as part of our standard Richmond removal service. We are familiar with the application requirements, lead times and fees of the relevant London borough council and will handle the entire process for you.

We recommend contacting us as early as possible after your exchange of contracts to allow sufficient time for the parking suspension application. Most London borough councils require a minimum of five to ten working days for suspension applications, and popular moving dates can see parking restrictions on particular streets already in place, requiring us to find an alternative solution.
