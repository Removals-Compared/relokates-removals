Professional man and van — not just any van

The man and van market is one of the most varied in the removals industry. At one end, you have professional, insured operators with proper vehicles and trained crew. At the other end, you have individuals with a Transit van and no insurance, no training and no accountability. The difference matters enormously when something goes wrong — and in the removals industry, things occasionally do go wrong.

Relokates man and van is a fully professional service. Our drivers are trained, our vehicles are properly maintained and loaded, and every job is covered by the same comprehensive goods-in-transit and public liability insurance that we carry on all our other removal services. When you book a man and van through Relokates, you are booking a professional removal service in a smaller format — not a casual favour from someone with access to a van.

When man and van is the right choice

Our man and van service is ideal for the following situations:

  • Studio and one-bedroom moves: Small properties with limited furniture and belongings are often more efficiently and cost-effectively moved with a man and van than a full removal service with a large vehicle and multiple crew members.
  • Student relocations: Moving from student accommodation to a new house share, from university to a first flat, or back home at the end of term. Student moves are typically light on heavy furniture and heavy on boxes, which suits the man and van format perfectly.
  • Single item deliveries and collections: If you have bought a large item of furniture on eBay or Facebook Marketplace, or need a single bulky item moved from one address to another, our man and van is the most practical and cost-effective solution.
  • Office moves for very small businesses: A sole trader, small partnership or micro-business with a single room office can often be relocated efficiently with a man and van rather than a full commercial removal service.
  • Partial moves: If you are moving some items to a new property while leaving others at the current address, a man and van gives you the flexibility to move exactly what you need moved without paying for a full removal service.
  • Same-day and short-notice moves: Our man and van service is more flexible on scheduling than our full removal service. We can often accommodate same-day or next-day bookings for smaller jobs, particularly during quieter periods.

What does man and van include?

Every Relokates man and van booking includes a professional, trained driver and an appropriately sized vehicle for your load. Depending on the size of your move, we may send a second crew member to assist with loading and carrying. All moves include our standard goods-in-transit insurance and the same care and professionalism you would expect from our full removal service.

We carry basic packing materials on every van — furniture blankets and strapping to protect your items during transit. For larger man and van jobs with significant amounts of fragile items, we recommend booking our packing materials option in advance so that everything is properly protected.

Pricing for man and van

Man and van pricing is based on the size of the vehicle required, the distance of the move, the number of crew members needed and the estimated duration. For short local moves, we may price on a fixed-fee basis. For longer or more complex jobs, we price based on an hourly or half-day rate. All pricing is confirmed as a fixed figure before your move — there are no surprise charges at the end of the job.
