Man and van vs full removal service - which do you need?

The right service depends on the volume of your belongings, the distance of your move and your budget. Here is a practical guide:

Choose man and van if...

You are moving a studio flat or one-bedroom property. You have limited furniture and mainly boxes. You need a single large item collected and delivered. You are a student moving between terms. Your move fits in a single van load.

Choose full removal service if...

You are moving a two-bedroom property or larger. You have a significant amount of furniture. You have specialist items such as a piano or large appliances. You want a full packing service included. You are moving long distance or internationally.

Not sure which you need?

Call us on 07359 724844 and describe your move. We will tell you honestly which service fits your needs and give you a fixed price for both options so you can compare.

Student moves - what you need to know

Student moves are one of the most common man and van requests we receive, particularly in London, Chelmsford and Canterbury. Here is what makes student moves different and how we handle them:

Term-end timing

June and September are the busiest periods for student moves. Book at least 2 to 3 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date and time.

University halls access restrictions

Many university halls have specific move-out time windows and vehicle access restrictions. Always confirm these with your accommodation office and pass the details to us when booking.

Storage between terms

If you need to store belongings over the summer rather than transport them home, our storage service is available from one week. We collect from your halls, store securely and redeliver at the start of the new term.

Not all man and van services are the same

The man and van market is unregulated.
