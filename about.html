Our story

About Relokates Removals

A professional, fully insured removal company built on honesty, care and a genuine commitment to making every move as smooth as possible across 475 locations.

Who we are

Relokates Removals is a professional, fully insured removal company covering over 475 locations across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex. We provide house removals, office relocations, professional packing, secure storage, man and van, luxury removals, house clearance and specialist international removals including the London to Dubai corridor.

Built on a simple principle

We built Relokates on a simple premise: the removals industry could do better. Better communication, better reliability, better care for customers' belongings and complete transparency about pricing. We have held to that premise on every single move we have carried out, and it is the foundation of everything we do.

Our team understands that moving house or relocating a business is one of the most stressful experiences a person can go through. We treat that responsibility seriously. Every item we handle is treated with the same care and respect we would give our own belongings.

Our coverage

Relokates is proud to offer one of the most comprehensive coverage networks of any independent removal company in the South East. Our 475 locations span all 33 London boroughs, over 65 towns in Essex, 80 locations across Kent and 55 towns in West Sussex.

Beyond our UK coverage, Relokates is a specialist in the London to Dubai international removal corridor: one of the UK's most active international removal routes. Our international team manages the complete end-to-end process from packing your UK property to delivery at your Dubai address.

Our values

Honesty: Fixed-price quotes. The amount we quote is what you pay. Always.

Care: Every item handled as if it were irreplaceable.

Reliability: We arrive when we say. We do what we say. We communicate proactively.

Professionalism: Uniformed, background-checked, trained to a consistent standard on every job.

Accountability: We stand behind our work. If something goes wrong, we deal with it promptly and fairly.
