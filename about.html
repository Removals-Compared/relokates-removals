Our story

About Relokates Removals

A professional, fully insured removal company built on honesty, care and a genuine commitment to making every move as smooth as possible — across 475 locations in London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex.

Who we are

Relokates Removals is a professional, fully insured removal company covering over 475 locations across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex. We provide house removals, office relocations, professional packing, secure storage, man and van, luxury removals, house clearance and specialist international removals including the London to Dubai corridor.

We built Relokates on a simple premise: that the removals industry could do better. Better communication, better reliability, better care for customers' belongings and complete transparency about pricing. We have held to that premise on every single move we have carried out, and it is the foundation of everything we do.

Our team is made up of removal professionals who understand that moving house or relocating a business is one of the most stressful experiences a person can go through. We treat that responsibility seriously. Every item we handle is treated with the same care and respect we would give our own belongings. Every commitment we make to a customer is one we intend to keep.

Our coverage area

Relokates is proud to offer one of the most comprehensive coverage networks of any independent removal company in the South East of England. Our 475 locations span all 33 London boroughs, over 65 towns and cities across Essex, 80 locations across Kent and 55 towns and villages in West Sussex.

Rather than simply listing our coverage areas as a long list of place names, we have built a dedicated, genuinely informative page for every single location we serve. Each of those pages is written with real local knowledge — the specific access challenges in that town, the typical property types, local parking restrictions, nearby storage facilities and honest local pricing context. This is not boilerplate content generated to fill space. It is information that genuinely helps our customers plan a smoother move.

Beyond our UK coverage, Relokates is a specialist in the London to Dubai international removal corridor. Our international team handles the complete end-to-end process from packing your UK property through to customs coordination, secure specialist shipping to the UAE and destination support at your Dubai address. International removals require a different level of expertise and planning, and that specialist knowledge sets us apart from general removal companies who offer international services as an afterthought.

Our values

Everything we do at Relokates is guided by five core values that inform how we treat our customers, how we train our team and how we approach every move we carry out.

Honesty: We are completely transparent about our pricing. The fixed-price quote we provide is the price you pay. We do not add hidden charges on moving day, and we do not use misleading base rates to make our initial quote look competitive before adding costs later. What we quote is what you pay.

Care: Every item we handle is treated as if it were irreplaceable. Our crew is trained in proper wrapping techniques, careful loading practices and methodical placement at the destination. We take moving your belongings as seriously as you do.

Reliability: We arrive when we say we will. We do what we say we are going to do. We communicate proactively when anything changes. In an industry where reliability is sometimes treated as optional, we consider it non-negotiable.

Professionalism: Our team is uniformed, background-checked and trained to a consistent standard. Every member of the Relokates crew represents the company and the values we have built the business on. We hold our team to the same standard on every job, whether it is a small man and van move in Grays or a complex international relocation to Dubai.

Accountability: We stand behind our work. If something goes wrong — and in the removals industry, very occasionally it does — we deal with it promptly, fairly and without making our customers fight for what they deserve. Our comprehensive insurance means that in the extremely unlikely event of damage, the process of making things right is straightforward.
