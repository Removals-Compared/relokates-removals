Our story

About Relokates Removals

A professional, fully insured removal company built on honesty, care and a genuine commitment to making every move as smooth as possible across 661 locations.

Who we are

Built on one simple principle

Relokates Removals was built on a single principle: treat every customer's belongings as if they were our own, be completely honest about pricing, and do exactly what we say we are going to do.

We cover over 661 locations across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex. Whether you are moving a studio flat in Grays, a large family home from Sevenoaks to Chichester, a professional office in Canary Wharf, or relocating your entire household from the UK to Dubai, Relokates manages every detail from the first call to the final box placed in your new property.

Our values

Honesty

Fixed-price quotes. The amount we quote is what you pay. Always. No additions on moving day.

Care

Every item handled as if it were irreplaceable. Protective wrapping, careful loading and precise placement as standard.

Reliability

We arrive when we say. We do what we say. We communicate proactively before and on moving day.

Professionalism

Uniformed, background-checked crew trained to a consistent standard on every job we carry out.

Accountability

We stand behind our work. If something goes wrong, we deal with it promptly and fairly.

Transparency

No hidden charges, no small print surprises. What you see is what you pay.

Our coverage

Relokates is proud to offer one of the most comprehensive coverage networks of any independent removal company in the South East. Our 661 locations span all 33 London boroughs, over 65 towns in Essex, 80 locations across Kent and 55 towns in West Sussex.

Beyond our UK coverage, Relokates is a specialist in the London to Dubai international removal corridor. Our international team manages the complete end-to-end process from packing your UK property to delivery at your Dubai address.

Fully insured on every move

Comprehensive goods-in-transit insurance covering the full replacement value of your belongings and full public liability insurance are active on every single removal we carry out. Proof of insurance documents are available on request before you book.
