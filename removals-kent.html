Removal company covering all of Kent

Kent is one of the most geographically varied counties in England: the Garden of England in name and character, combining some of the South East's most sought-after countryside and coastal locations with significant urban and commuter communities along its northern corridor. Relokates covers 80 locations across Kent, from the Medway towns of Rochester, Chatham, Gillingham and Strood in the north-west, through the county town of Maidstone and the cathedral city of Canterbury, to the coastal towns of Whitstable, Margate, Ramsgate and Folkestone in the east, and the High Weald market towns of Tonbridge, Tunbridge Wells and Sevenoaks in the west.

Kent is one of our most active operating areas. We carry out a very high volume of London to Kent relocations: families and professionals leaving the capital for more space, better value and excellent schools while maintaining a manageable commute via the county's excellent rail network. The opening of the Elizabeth line and improvements to Southeastern services have made many Kent towns even more accessible from central London, driving sustained property demand and removal activity across the county.

Key Kent towns we serve

Maidstone: Kent's county town and one of the South East's major commercial centres. A diverse mix of period properties, Victorian terraces and modern new-build developments creates high removal activity throughout the year.

Canterbury: A UNESCO World Heritage city and one of England's most historically significant locations. The university drives significant student and professional removal activity alongside a strong permanent resident market.

Gravesend and Dartford: North Kent's key commuter towns, both benefiting from Elizabeth line connectivity and strong transport links to London. Among the most popular destinations for London relocations in the county.

Sevenoaks: One of Kent's most prestigious and expensive areas, popular with City workers for its fast Southeastern services to London Bridge and Charing Cross. Family homes here command premium prices.

Medway towns: Rochester, Chatham, Gillingham and Strood together form one of the South East's largest urban areas. The variety of property types and price points makes the Medway area one of our busiest operating zones in Kent.

Tonbridge and Tunbridge Wells: Two of West Kent's most desirable addresses. Tonbridge offers excellent schools and a market town feel. Tunbridge Wells has a distinctive character with the famous Pantiles and a thriving independent retail scene.

London to Kent removals

The London to Kent corridor is one of our most active routes. We carry out dozens of London to Kent removals every month across a wide range of routes: South London to Sevenoaks, East London to Gravesend and Dartford, Central London to Canterbury, North London to Maidstone. We provide competitive fixed-price quotes for all London to Kent routes and have deep knowledge of the access, parking and logistics requirements at both the London and Kent ends of each move.
