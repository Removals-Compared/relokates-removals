Secure storage

Secure storage solutions across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex

Fully insured, fixed-price service covering 475 locations across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex. Call 07359 724844 for a free quote.

Storage that works around your move

The timing of a property move rarely works out perfectly. Completion delays, renovation works, property chains that collapse and restart, new builds that overrun their handover date — all of these situations can create a gap between leaving your current property and being able to move into your new one. Relokates secure storage is designed to bridge that gap seamlessly, with flexible terms, comprehensive insurance and a collection and delivery service that means you never have to manage the logistics yourself.

Our storage is not just for completion gaps. Many of our customers use storage as part of a planned downsizing, to hold items during a major renovation, to store the contents of a property being prepared for sale, or simply to manage the volume of belongings during a large move that takes place in stages.

What our storage service includes

  • Collection from your property: Our crew collects your belongings from your current address, packs them if required and transports them securely to our storage facility. You do not need to arrange transport or physically visit the storage location.
  • Secure, monitored storage: Your belongings are stored in a secure, dry, monitored facility. Access is controlled and the facility is protected by CCTV and alarm systems. Your items are not accessible to other customers or members of the public.
  • Full insurance: All items in storage are covered by our comprehensive insurance throughout the storage period. The level of cover is based on the declared value of your belongings at the time of collection.
  • Flexible terms: We offer storage from a minimum of one week with no maximum term. You are not locked into a long-term contract. When you are ready for delivery, we give you a confirmed date and deliver your belongings to your new property.
  • Delivery to your new address: When you are ready, we deliver everything from storage to your new property. If you have booked our removal service, the delivery from storage is coordinated as part of your overall move.
  • Inventory management: All items going into storage are inventoried. You have a complete record of what is in storage and where it is located within the facility.

Common situations where storage helps

Completion day delays: You have handed over the keys to your current home but your solicitor calls to say completion on your new property has been delayed by a day or two. Rather than living out of a hotel with all your belongings in a van, we can store everything securely while you wait for completion to proceed.

New build delays: New build handover dates are frequently pushed back. If your developer advises that your new home will not be ready on the agreed date, our storage service allows you to vacate your current property on schedule without being forced into temporary accommodation with nowhere for your furniture.

Renovation before moving in: If you are planning a significant renovation at your new property before you move in — a new kitchen, bathroom renovation, rewiring or replastering — it often makes sense to keep your furniture and belongings in storage until the work is complete. This protects your belongings from dust and damage during building works and gives the tradespeople the space they need to work efficiently.

Downsizing: If you are moving to a smaller property, you may not be able to take everything with you immediately. Storage gives you time to decide which items to keep, sell or donate without having to make those decisions under the pressure of moving day.

How long can I store my belongings?

There is no maximum storage term. Some of our customers store items for a few days during a completion gap. Others store for several months during an extended renovation project. A small number of our customers use ongoing storage for items they want to keep but cannot accommodate in their current property. We accommodate all of these situations with flexible, month-to-month terms after the initial period.

Storage costs are based on the volume of items stored and the duration of storage. We will give you a clear weekly or monthly cost as part of your quote. There are no setup fees or hidden charges. Collection and delivery costs are quoted separately and are typically based on the same pricing as our standard removal service for equivalent distances.
