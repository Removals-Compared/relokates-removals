Secure storage

Secure storage solutions across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex

Insured, monitored short and long-term storage. Ideal for completion gaps, renovation works, downsizing or any gap between addresses. Call 07359 724844.

Storage that works around your move

The timing of a property move rarely works out perfectly. Completion delays, renovation works, property chains: all of these situations can create a gap between leaving your current property and being able to move into your new one. Relokates secure storage bridges that gap seamlessly, with flexible terms, comprehensive insurance and a collection and delivery service that means you never have to manage the logistics yourself.

What our storage service includes

  • Collection from your property: we collect, pack if required and transport everything securely
  • Secure, monitored storage: CCTV and alarm protected, access controlled
  • Full insurance throughout: your belongings are covered for their full declared value
  • Flexible terms: minimum one week, no maximum, no long-term contracts
  • Delivery to your new address: we deliver everything when you are ready
  • Full inventory management: complete record of everything in storage

Common situations where storage helps

Completion day delays: Your solicitor calls to say completion is delayed by a day or two. We store everything securely while you wait.

New build delays: Your developer has pushed back the handover date. Store your belongings safely until the property is ready.

Renovation before moving in: Keep your furniture and belongings protected in storage while building works are completed.

Downsizing: Store items you cannot immediately accommodate while you decide what to keep, sell or donate.
