Saturday vs Sunday - which works best?

Saturday Most popular

The default weekend choice - most working professionals book Saturday because it leaves Sunday clear for unpacking.

  • Standard 8am-10am start window
  • Earlier 7am starts available for longer-distance moves
  • Books fastest - recommend 3-4 weeks notice
  • Last Saturday of the month is heavily booked, especially May-September

Sunday

The hidden gem - far less booked than Saturdays, with better last-minute availability.

  • Often available with 48-72 hours notice
  • Quieter roads = faster moves, especially in London
  • Easier for parking in central areas
  • Useful if your purchase or rental completes Friday afternoon

Bank holidays

We operate every bank holiday Monday at standard pricing. Bank holiday weekends are particularly useful because they give you an extra unpacking day before you return to work. The main weekends to plan for:

Easter weekend4-day weekend. Books out 6 weeks ahead.
Early May bank holiday3-day weekend. Popular with families before half-term.
Late May bank holiday3-day weekend. Aligned with school half-term - busiest weekend.
Late August bank holiday3-day weekend. Back-to-school move favourite.
Christmas / Boxing DayQuieter weekend. Last-minute availability often possible.

Why weekend moves work better for working professionals

No annual leave used. An average house move costs 1-2 days of work productivity if done midweek. A Saturday move costs none. For a salaried professional this is often worth £400-600 of effective saving on top of the move cost.

Add Sunday unpacking, and you arrive at work Monday with your new home already functional. This is the calculation most of our weekend customers are making.

Booking tips for weekend slots

  • Book Saturdays 3-4 weeks ahead - especially the last Saturday of the month, which is the single most heavily booked slot in our calendar
  • Sundays often have last-minute availability - if you missed the Saturday window, ask about Sunday before assuming you have to wait two weeks
  • Avoid bank holiday weekends in May and August for last-minute bookings - these are 6 weeks ahead minimum
  • Friday afternoon completion? Book your move for Sunday - gives you a day to clean and prep the new property
  • Long-distance weekend moves - book a 7am start to ensure delivery before nightfall
