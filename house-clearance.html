Responsible disposal - our commitment

We are committed to minimising waste going to landfill. Here is where cleared items go:

Furniture in good condition

Offered to local charities and community groups across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex. We have established relationships with charities that collect regularly.

Clothing, books and household items

Sorted and donated to charity shops. Books in good condition go to school and library donation programmes where possible.

Electrical items

All electrical items go through approved WEEE recycling. We never dispose of electricals in general waste - it is illegal and environmentally harmful.

Licensed waste disposal

Relokates is a licensed waste carrier. All waste disposal is fully compliant with UK waste regulations. We can provide our waste carrier licence number on request - important if you are instructing a clearance on behalf of an estate.

Important guidance

Choosing a responsible house clearance company

The house clearance market contains a significant number of operators who are not licensed waste carriers. Engaging an unlicensed operator means your waste may be fly-tipped illegally - and as the property owner or executor, you can be held responsible for illegal waste disposal even if you did not carry it out yourself.

Before engaging any house clearance company, ask to see their waste carrier licence number and verify it on the Environment Agency register. Relokates is a licensed waste carrier and we can provide our licence number before you book.
