A house clearance service built on respect

A house clearance is often required at a difficult time. The death of a loved one, a move into care, a family home being sold after decades. the circumstances that lead to a house clearance are frequently emotional and demanding. At Relokates, we approach every house clearance with the sensitivity these situations deserve. Our team works at a pace that suits you and treats every item in the property with care and respect.

We approach every clearance with respect and sensitivity. Our trained team takes great care with every item in the property, understanding that belongings often carry personal and sentimental significance.

When house clearance is needed

  • Bereavement clearances, handled with particular care and sensitivity, at your pace
  • Downsizing, take what you need to your new home, have the rest managed responsibly
  • Property preparation for sale, empty properties photograph better and sell faster
  • Care home moves, when a family member moves into care, we work with families sensitively
  • Rental property clearances, bringing properties back to a lettable condition efficiently

Our team handles every item in your property with care and discretion. We work at the pace you need and ensure nothing is removed that you have not agreed should go.

Responsible disposal and recycling

We are committed to minimising waste going to landfill. Furniture in good condition is offered to local charities. Clothing, books and household items are sorted for donation. Electrical items go through approved WEEE recycling. Only items that genuinely cannot be reused are disposed of as waste. We are licensed waste carriers and all disposal is fully compliant with UK waste regulations.
