Who we are

The removal company families and businesses across the UK trust

Relokates Removals was built on a single principle that has guided every one of our moves since we began: treat every customer's belongings as if they were our own, be completely honest about pricing, and do exactly what we say we are going to do. That principle has not changed, and it is the reason our customers come back to us, recommend us to friends and family, and leave us five-star reviews across Google and other platforms.

We cover over 475 locations across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex. Whether you are moving a studio flat in Grays, a large family home from Sevenoaks to Chichester, a professional office in Canary Wharf, or relocating your entire household from the UK to Dubai, Relokates manages every detail from the first call to the final box placed in your new property.

Our fully insured, fixed-price approach means you always know exactly what you are paying before moving day arrives. There are no hidden charges for stairs, parking delays, heavy items or extra time. What we quote is what you pay — every single time.

We are proud to offer one of the most comprehensive coverage networks of any independent removal company in the South East. Our 475 locations span all 33 London boroughs, over 65 towns in Essex, 80 locations across Kent and 55 locations in West Sussex. Every area we serve has its own dedicated page on our website, written with genuine local knowledge about the specific challenges and characteristics of moving in that area.

Beyond domestic moves, Relokates is one of the UK's leading specialists on the London to Dubai international relocation corridor. Our international team handles the entire process from professional packing in your UK property through to customs coordination, specialist secure shipping and complete destination support in Dubai and across the UAE. It is a complex process that requires genuine expertise — and that expertise is what we provide.
