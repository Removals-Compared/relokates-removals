Who we are

The removal company families and businesses across the UK trust

Relokates Removals was built on a single principle that has guided every one of our moves since we began: treat every customer's belongings as if they were our own, be completely honest about pricing, and do exactly what we say we are going to do.

We cover over 475 locations across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex. Whether you are moving a studio flat in Grays, a large family home from Sevenoaks to Chichester, a professional office in Canary Wharf, or relocating your entire household from the UK to Dubai, Relokates manages every detail from the first call to the final box placed in your new property.
