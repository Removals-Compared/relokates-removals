Removal company covering all of West Sussex

West Sussex is one of the most attractive counties in the South East: a combination of the South Downs National Park, the Sussex coast, the High Weald and accessible market towns that make it an increasingly popular destination for families and professionals relocating from London and other parts of the South East. Relokates covers 55 towns and locations across West Sussex as part of our 661-location network.

The county divides broadly into three zones. In the north, Crawley and Horsham are significant commuter towns with strong rail connections to London. The central belt through Haywards Heath and Burgess Hill serves a population that is almost entirely London-commuter facing. The coastal strip from Shoreham-by-Sea through Worthing, Littlehampton and Bognor Regis to Chichester attracts both retirees and younger families seeking coastal living.

Key West Sussex towns we serve

Chichester: West Sussex's county city and one of the South of England's most historically and culturally significant locations. The Cathedral, Festival Theatre and yacht harbour at Chichester Marina create a distinctive character. Property here is predominantly period housing and commands premium prices.

Crawley: A new town built in the post-war period and now a significant commercial centre adjacent to Gatwick Airport. The mix of new-build housing and older estates across Crawley's many neighbourhoods creates varied removal requirements.

Horsham: One of West Sussex's most desirable market towns with a strong independent retail scene, excellent schools and good rail connectivity to London. Popular with professional families.

Worthing: West Sussex's largest town by population and one of the county's most popular coastal destinations. A growing arts scene and improving connectivity have attracted younger residents alongside the established retired population.

Haywards Heath: A key commuter town with fast trains to London Bridge and Gatwick. One of the most popular destinations for London families relocating to West Sussex.
