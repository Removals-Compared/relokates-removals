What is included in your Chelmsford removal

Every Relokates house removal in Chelmsford includes the following as standard with no additions on moving day:

  • Pre-move planning call: A detailed discussion of your move before moving day confirming timings, vehicle access at both properties, parking requirements and any specialist items or challenges specific to your move.
  • Full protective wrapping: All furniture, mirrors, framed pictures, electrical equipment and fragile items are wrapped in protective blankets, bubble wrap or specialist covers. We do not cut corners on protection and wrap every item before it leaves your property.
  • Careful, methodical loading: Our crew uses professional loading techniques that maximise stability and protection during transit. Fragile items are always loaded last and secured appropriately so that everything arrives exactly as it was packed.
  • Furniture disassembly and reassembly: We disassemble beds, wardrobes, flat-pack furniture and other items that need to be taken apart for the move, and reassemble everything at your new property as standard.
  • Precise placement at destination: We place every item exactly where you want it in your new property. We do not simply stack boxes in the hallway and consider the job done.
  • Complete debris removal: All protective materials, wrapping and packing materials are removed at the end of the move, leaving your new property clean and ready to live in from day one.

Moving checklist for Chelmsford

When planning your move to or from Chelmsford, here are some important tasks to complete well ahead of moving day:

  • Check A12 and A127 conditions on moving day for routes to and from London
  • Book your removal early given high demand for Essex commuter belt moves
  • Apply for parking suspensions if moving in any town centre or restricted street property
  • Contact Essex County Council for address changes and council tax updates
  • Notify your employer of your address change well in advance of moving day

Our team will provide specific advice relevant to your move during the quotation process. We have extensive experience with removals across Chelmsford and Essex and can advise on any local considerations that apply to your specific move.
