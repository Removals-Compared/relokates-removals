Whether you are moving within Crawley, relocating elsewhere in West Sussex, moving to London or making a longer distance move, our team manages every aspect from first call to final box.

Property in Crawley

Crawley was built as a new town and has a predominance of post-war housing across its many distinct neighbourhoods: Ifield, Pound Hill, Three Bridges, Maidenbower and Broadfield each have their own character. Modern new-build development continues on the town's outskirts.

Whatever the size or type of property you are moving in Crawley, Relokates has the vehicles, equipment and expertise to manage the job correctly. We carry out a thorough pre-move assessment before providing a fixed-price quote with no surprises on moving day.

Local knowledge: access and parking in Crawley

Crawley's new town layout means some cul-de-sac estate roads can be challenging for larger vehicles. The neighbourhoods each have different access characteristics that our team is familiar with.

This local knowledge means we arrive prepared on moving day rather than discovering challenges for the first time. For properties in Crawley that require parking suspensions, we handle the application with the relevant West Sussex council on your behalf.

Moving from Crawley to London

Relocations between Crawley and London are a regular part of our work. London to West Sussex moves: families leaving the capital for space, schools and coastal or countryside living: and the reverse journey for those returning to the city are among our most common routes. We provide competitive fixed-price quotes for all Crawley to London corridors and cover all 33 London boroughs at the other end.
