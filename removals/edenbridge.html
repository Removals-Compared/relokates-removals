Moving checklist for Edenbridge

When planning your move to or from Edenbridge, complete these important tasks well ahead of moving day to avoid last-minute complications:

  • Book parking suspensions early for any town centre or restricted street properties
  • Check the M25, A3 and M3 for planned closures on your moving day
  • Contact Surrey County Council for address changes and council tax
  • Research school catchment areas carefully if relocating with children
  • Update your driving licence and vehicle registration to your new address
