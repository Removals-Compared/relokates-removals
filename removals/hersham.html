About Hersham

Surrey is one of the most affluent counties in the UK, with a mix of commuter towns, village communities and leafy suburbs. Property ranges from Victorian terraces in Redhill and Reigate to executive detached homes in Weybridge, Esher and Virginia Water.

Surrey has excellent rail connections into London Waterloo, Victoria and London Bridge, making it a popular choice for London commuters seeking more space and a better quality of life.
