Luxury removals

Luxury and specialist removals for high-value items across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex

White-glove service for fine art, grand pianos, antiques, high-value furniture and specialist items. Bespoke crating and specialist insurance available. Call 07359 724844.

When standard care is not enough

Some belongings require expertise that goes beyond the standard removal process. Fine art, antiques, grand pianos, vintage wines and high-value furniture demand specialist knowledge, specialist materials and specialist handling. Our luxury team is trained beyond our standard crew and uses specialist equipment on every luxury removal we carry out.

What we move

  • Fine art and paintings: acid-free materials, bespoke crating, specialist handling
  • Antiques and collectibles: assessed individually with appropriate packing techniques
  • Grand and upright pianos: specialist equipment, trained piano crew, disassembly where required
  • Luxury furniture: designer pieces from B&B Italia, Minotti, Fendi Casa and similar brands
  • Sculptures: bespoke crating for stone, bronze and glass pieces
  • Wine collections: temperature-controlled handling and storage available
  • Safes: specialist equipment for all sizes and weights
