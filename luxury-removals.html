When standard care is not enough

Some belongings require a level of care and expertise that goes beyond the standard removal process. Fine art, antiques, grand pianos, vintage wines, high-value jewellery and watches, designer furniture and other luxury or specialist items demand specialist knowledge, specialist materials and specialist handling techniques. A crew member who knows how to load a sofa efficiently does not necessarily know how to handle an oil painting, move a concert grand piano or pack a collection of rare ceramics.

Relokates luxury removal service is built around the specific requirements of high-value, fragile or specialist items. Our luxury team is trained beyond our standard crew training, uses specialist equipment and materials, and approaches every item they handle with the care and respect it deserves. We work with private individuals, interior designers, auction houses, art dealers and estate agents across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex.

What we move

  • Fine art and paintings: Oil paintings, watercolours, prints and other framed artwork require specialist handling, appropriate packing and careful climate management. We use acid-free materials throughout, bespoke crating where required, and specialist handling techniques that protect both the artwork and its frame.
  • Antiques and collectibles: Antique furniture, sculpture, ceramics, silverware, clocks, books and other collectibles require an understanding of the specific fragilities of each piece and appropriate packing techniques. We assess each item individually before deciding on the most appropriate handling method.
  • Grand and upright pianos: Piano removal is a specialist skill. A concert grand piano can weigh up to 600kg and requires specific equipment, specialist disassembly in some cases, and a crew that understands how to manage the weight and balance safely. We carry specialist piano moving equipment including piano boards, skates and lifting straps, and our piano crew are specifically trained for this work.
  • Luxury furniture: Designer furniture from brands such as B&B Italia, Minotti, Fendi Casa, Roche Bobois and similar requires handling that goes beyond the standard approach. We use appropriate protective materials, take extra care with lacquered, upholstered and glass surfaces and ensure that delivery and placement is as careful as collection.
  • Sculptures and three-dimensional art: Sculptures in stone, bronze, glass and other materials each have specific handling requirements. We assess each piece individually and create appropriate packing solutions, including bespoke crating for larger or particularly fragile works.
  • Wine collections: Valuable wine collections require temperature-controlled handling and storage. We can arrange climate-controlled transport and storage for wine collections where required.
  • Safes: Safes can be extremely heavy and require specialist equipment for safe removal. We have experience moving safes of various sizes and weights across all our coverage areas.

Our white-glove approach

The term white-glove is used frequently in the luxury removal and logistics industry, but its meaning varies. At Relokates, white-glove means the following in practice: every item is assessed individually before being handled; packing materials are selected specifically for each item; no item leaves your property without being fully protected; placement at the destination is careful, precise and unhurried; and our crew understands that for many of these items, replacement is not simply a matter of buying the same thing again.

Insurance for high-value items

Standard goods-in-transit insurance has limits on individual item values that may not be adequate for high-value artwork, jewellery, antiques or other specialist items. For luxury removals, we recommend a specialist declared value insurance policy that provides cover appropriate to the actual value of the items being moved. We can advise on specialist insurance options as part of your luxury removal consultation.
