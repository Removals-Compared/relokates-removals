Removal costs vary depending on the size of your property, the distance of your move, the volume of your belongings, whether you require packing services, and the access conditions at both properties. A three-bedroom house move from London to Essex would typically cost between £650 and £950. A large five-bedroom family home from London to West Sussex would typically range from £1,200 to £2,000. Packing and services

Services questions

Yes. Our professional packing service is available as a full pack, part-pack or materials-only option. Full pack means our team arrives the day before or on moving day and packs your entire home using premium double-walled boxes, bubble wrap, specialist wardrobe boxes, mirror packs, TV packs and protective furniture covers. Part-pack means we pack the items you feel less confident packing yourself: typically fragile items, artwork, electronics and specialist pieces. Materials-only means we supply you with all the packaging materials you need for a professional self-pack. Professional packing significantly reduces the risk of damage during transit and is particularly recommended for fragile items, antiques, artwork, electronics and high-value belongings.
Yes. Relokates offers secure, insured short and long-term storage. This is ideal for completion gaps, renovation works underway at your new property, downsizing where you have more belongings than space, or any situation where you need your belongings kept safely between addresses. We can collect your belongings from your current property and deliver them to your new address when you are ready. All stored items are covered by our comprehensive insurance throughout.
We recommend booking as early as possible, particularly for moves during peak periods. Peak periods include the end of June, July and August, school holidays, bank holidays, and end-of-month dates when many property completions happen simultaneously. During these times, removal companies across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex are in extremely high demand and dates fill up quickly. For a standard move, aim to book at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance. For moves during peak periods, 8 weeks is preferable. For international moves to Dubai, allow at least 8 to 12 weeks to allow time for customs coordination and shipping arrangements.
International removals

Dubai and international questions

Yes. Relokates is a specialist in the London to Dubai international removal corridor. We handle the complete end-to-end process including professional packing at your UK property, full customs documentation for UAE import, secure specialist shipping by sea or air freight depending on your requirements and timeline, and destination support at your Dubai address. We work with trusted, established freight partners who operate regular services between the UK and the UAE. Our international team has deep experience in the specific requirements of UK to Dubai relocations and will guide you through every step of the process.
Transit times for UK to Dubai removal shipments depend on the method chosen. Sea freight in a dedicated container typically takes 20 to 28 days from UK port to Dubai port, plus customs clearance time at the Dubai end which typically adds 5 to 10 working days. Air freight is significantly faster: typically 5 to 10 days door to door: but is substantially more expensive and is generally only used for smaller, high-value or time-critical shipments. We will discuss the best option for your specific situation and timeline during your free consultation.
The UAE has strict regulations on what can be imported. Items that cannot be shipped to Dubai include alcohol, pork products and pork-derived items, narcotics and controlled substances, weapons and ammunition, politically sensitive material, and certain medications that are controlled in the UAE but not in the UK. We provide a full prohibited and restricted items list as part of our international consultation and will advise you on any items that may require special documentation or alternative arrangements. Our international team is well versed in UAE customs regulations and will ensure your shipment complies fully.
