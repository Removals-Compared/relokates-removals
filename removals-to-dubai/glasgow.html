Glasgow Airport provides connections to Dubai via London Heathrow and other European hubs.

Getting from Glasgow to Dubai

Glasgow Airport operates connecting services to Dubai via London Heathrow, Amsterdam and other European hubs. Emirates provides daily direct services from Heathrow for Glasgow passengers travelling south. Sea freight from Scotland departs from the Port of Grangemouth on the Firth of Forth, with transit times to Dubai of approximately 6 to 9 weeks.

Sea freight from Glasgow to Dubai takes approximately 5 to 8 weeks door to door and is the most cost-effective option for full household removals. Air freight takes 5 to 10 days and is suitable for time-critical or smaller shipments. We advise on the best option for your specific timeline, volume and budget during the pre-move consultation.

Glasgow and Dubai — the professional connection

The city's engineering and construction heritage creates strong connections with the Gulf's ongoing infrastructure development, and many Glasgow professionals have built long careers rotating between Scottish and UAE postings.

For professionals from Glasgow considering their next career move, Dubai provides an unmatched combination of financial reward, lifestyle and professional opportunity.

UAE customs and documentation

A valid UAE residence visa is required before personal effects can clear UAE Customs as a tax-free import. Your employer in Dubai will typically arrange your work permit and residence visa, but you should confirm the expected timeline as early as possible as this process can take several weeks. Relokates prepares all shipping documentation and UAE customs declarations on your behalf and coordinates with our Dubai customs brokers throughout the clearance process.

Items that cannot be shipped from Glasgow to Dubai include alcohol, pork products, narcotics, weapons, politically sensitive material and certain medications. We provide a complete prohibited and restricted items list during your pre-move consultation so nothing is left to chance at the Dubai end.

The Glasgow to Dubai moving timeline

Planning a removal from Glasgow to Dubai requires careful advance preparation across several months. The most important first step is securing your UAE residence visa, which your Dubai employer will typically arrange but which can take 4 to 8 weeks to process. Starting the visa application as early as possible is essential, as your personal effects cannot clear UAE Customs without a valid residence visa in place.

Once your visa application is underway, contact Relokates to arrange a pre-move survey at your Glasgow property. We assess the full volume of goods to be shipped, advise on what to bring to Dubai versus what to sell, donate or store in the UK, and provide a fixed-price quotation covering the complete door-to-door service. This survey can be done in person at your Glasgow property or via video call if your timeline is tight.

Most Glasgow-to-Dubai movers choose sea freight for the bulk of their household goods and complement this with a small air freight consignment of essential items needed in the first weeks in Dubai. The main sea freight container is loaded at your Glasgow property by our professional export packing team, transported to the UK port and loaded onto the vessel. Transit time from a UK port to Dubai is approximately 5 to 8 weeks depending on the routing and vessel schedule.

On arrival in Dubai, your goods are cleared through UAE Customs by our local partners and delivered to your new Dubai address. We coordinate every step of this process from our UK base, providing a single point of contact throughout and regular progress updates so you always know exactly where your belongings are.

Schools in Dubai for families relocating from Glasgow

Families relocating from Glasgow to Dubai with school-age children will find a wide selection of British curriculum schools that provide continuity with the English National Curriculum. Schools including GEMS Wellington Academy, Repton School Dubai, Kings School Dubai and the British School Al Khaleej follow the National Curriculum and offer GCSEs and A-Levels, ensuring a seamless educational transition for children arriving from Glasgow.

School places at the most popular British curriculum schools in Dubai are in high demand and we strongly recommend researching and applying to schools well before your planned departure from Glasgow. Many schools operate waiting lists and beginning the application process six months ahead of your intended move date is advisable. The KHDA (Knowledge and Human Development Authority) publishes annual inspection reports on all Dubai schools which are an invaluable resource when comparing options.

Moving checklist: Glasgow to Dubai

  • Secure your UAE residence visa well before your planned departure date from Glasgow
  • Allow 8 to 12 weeks from booking to delivery in Dubai for sea freight shipments
  • Obtain a complete inventory of all household goods for UAE Customs documentation
  • Identify and remove any prohibited items that cannot be shipped to the UAE
  • Arrange UAE private health insurance before your departure from Glasgow
  • Notify HMRC of your change of tax residency status well in advance
  • Arrange a UK mail forwarding service for the transition period
  • Confirm your Dubai lease or property purchase before booking packing
  • Research and apply to international schools in Dubai well ahead if moving with children
  • Open a UAE bank account as early as possible to facilitate the financial transition
