Sea freight from London to Dubai

Sea freight is the most cost-effective option for full household moves from London to Dubai. We offer dedicated container shipping: where your belongings occupy an entire 20-foot or 40-foot container: and shared container shipping for smaller volumes. Transit time from the UK to Dubai by sea is typically 20 to 28 days, with total door-to-door time including collection from your London property and delivery to your Dubai address running at 5 to 9 weeks depending on customs clearance and scheduling.

Air freight from London to Dubai

Air freight from London to Dubai is significantly faster: typically 5 to 10 days door to door: but substantially more expensive per cubic metre. We recommend air freight for small, high-value or time-critical shipments, or for customers who need specific items to arrive before the main sea freight shipment. We can arrange a split shipment: air freight for essential items and sea freight for the remainder: if your timeline requires it.

London removers to Dubai typically come from all borough types: from luxury Mayfair and Knightsbridge apartments to family homes in Bromley, Croydon and the outer boroughs.

Whatever the size or nature of your London property, we have the expertise and equipment to move its contents safely to Dubai. We use export-grade packing materials throughout: double-walled boxes, specialist fragile item packing, custom wooden crating for artwork and antiques, and protective covers for all furniture. Every item is inventoried and documented for customs purposes.

UAE customs: what you need to know

Importing household goods into the UAE requires compliance with UAE customs regulations. Key requirements include a detailed packing inventory with declared values, a copy of your UAE residency visa or entry permit, a copy of your passport, and a customs declaration form. Prohibited items include alcohol, pork products, narcotics, weapons, certain medications and politically sensitive material. We prepare all required documentation as part of our service and advise you on any items that may require special handling.
