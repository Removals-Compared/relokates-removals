Transport connections from Brighton

Brighton has outstanding rail connections to London Victoria and London Bridge in under an hour. Gatwick Airport is approximately 30 minutes by rail and provides extensive international connections including direct services to Dubai. The A23 and M23 connect Brighton to the M25.

Sea freight from Brighton to Dubai departs from UK ports and takes approximately 5 to 8 weeks door to door. Air freight takes 5 to 10 days and is available for urgent or smaller shipments. We advise on the most appropriate option during your pre-move consultation based on your timeline, volume and budget.

The Brighton to Dubai removal process

Our professional export packing team arrives at your Brighton property and packs everything using export-grade materials: double-walled boxes, bubble wrap, acid-free tissue paper and purpose-built crates for fragile or high-value items. Every item is individually listed and numbered on the shipping inventory for customs and tracking purposes.

We then transport your goods to the UK port or airport, manage the freight booking and container loading, prepare all UAE customs documentation, and coordinate with our Dubai partners for customs clearance and final delivery. Nothing is left to chance and you receive regular updates throughout the process.

Dubai areas popular with Brighton residents

Professionals relocating from Brighton to Dubai most commonly settle in Dubai Marina, Jumeirah, Downtown Dubai and the DIFC area depending on their workplace and lifestyle preferences. Dubai Marina offers modern high-rise apartments with direct beach access and a vibrant social scene. Jumeirah and Umm Suqeim provide villa communities popular with families relocating from Brighton with children, with proximity to several excellent British curriculum international schools.

For those relocating from Brighton for financial or technology sector roles, the DIFC and Business Bay areas are the natural choice, with premium apartments and proximity to the major financial institutions. Arabian Ranches and the Springs provide community living with pools, parks and a strong family environment at more accessible price points.

UAE customs and documentation

A valid UAE residence visa is required before personal effects can clear UAE Customs as a duty-free import. Your employer in Dubai will typically arrange your work permit and residence visa, but you should confirm the timeline early as this process can take several weeks. Relokates prepares all shipping documentation and customs declarations on your behalf and coordinates with our Dubai Customs brokers throughout.

Items that cannot be shipped from Brighton to Dubai include alcohol, pork products, narcotics, weapons, politically sensitive material and certain medications. We provide a complete prohibited and restricted items list during your pre-move consultation. Electrical items should be compatible with UAE voltage of 220 to 240 volts, which is the same as the UK standard.

Moving checklist: Brighton to Dubai

  • Secure your UAE residence visa well before your planned departure from Brighton
  • Allow 8 to 12 weeks from booking to delivery in Dubai for sea freight shipments
  • Obtain a full inventory of all household goods for UAE Customs documentation
  • Identify and remove any prohibited items that cannot be included in the shipment
  • Arrange UAE health insurance before your departure date from Brighton
  • Notify HMRC of your change of tax residency status
  • Arrange UK mail forwarding for the transition period after leaving Brighton
  • Confirm your Dubai lease or property purchase is complete before booking packing
  • Research and apply to international schools in Dubai well in advance if moving with children
  • Open a UAE bank account as early as possible to facilitate the financial transition
