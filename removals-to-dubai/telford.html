Sea freight from Telford to Dubai

Sea freight is the most cost-effective option for full household moves from Telford to Dubai. We offer dedicated container shipping — where your belongings occupy an entire 20-foot or 40-foot container — and shared container shipping for smaller volumes. Transit time from the UK to Dubai by sea is typically 20 to 28 days, with total door-to-door time including collection from your Telford property and delivery to your Dubai address running at 5 to 9 weeks depending on customs clearance and scheduling.

Air freight from Telford to Dubai

Air freight from Telford to Dubai is significantly faster — typically 5 to 10 days door to door — but substantially more expensive per cubic metre. We recommend air freight for small, high-value or time-critical shipments, or for customers who need specific items to arrive before the main sea freight shipment. We can arrange a split shipment — air freight for essential items and sea freight for the remainder — if your timeline requires it.

UAE customs — what you need to know

Importing household goods into the UAE requires compliance with UAE customs regulations. Key requirements include a detailed packing inventory with declared values, a copy of your UAE residency visa or entry permit, a copy of your passport, and a customs declaration form. Prohibited items include alcohol, pork products, narcotics, weapons, certain medications and politically sensitive material. We prepare all required documentation as part of our service and advise you on any items that may require special handling.
