Specialist international removals from the UK

International relocation is one of the most complex logistical challenges a household or business can face. Beyond the physical challenge of moving your belongings, you are navigating customs regulations, import duties, specialist shipping methods, documentation requirements and destination logistics in a country where the rules, processes and infrastructure may be entirely unfamiliar to you. At Relokates, international removals — particularly along the London to Dubai corridor — are a core part of what we do, not an afterthought bolted onto a domestic removal service.

We have built a specialist international team with deep expertise in UK outbound removals. Our team understands the specific requirements of major destination countries including the UAE, and works with a network of established, trusted freight partners to ensure your belongings are transported safely, compliantly and on schedule.

London to Dubai — our specialist corridor

The London to Dubai removal corridor is the single most active UK international removal route, driven by the high volume of British professionals, families and businesses relocating to the UAE each year. Dubai's business-friendly environment, tax advantages, world-class infrastructure and lifestyle have made it one of the most popular relocation destinations for UK residents, and that flow of people shows no sign of slowing.

Relokates has developed specialist expertise on this route. We understand the Dubai customs process in detail — what documentation is required, what the import regulations are, what items are restricted or prohibited, and what the realistic timeline looks like from packing in London to delivery at a Dubai address. This is not knowledge we have acquired from a manual. It is knowledge built from managing hundreds of London to Dubai relocations.

What our London to Dubai service includes

  • Pre-move consultation: We meet with you — in person or by video call — to discuss your move in detail. We cover the customs requirements, documentation checklist, prohibited items, shipping options and realistic timeline for your specific circumstances.
  • Professional packing: All items are professionally packed using specialist export-grade materials. Fragile items, electronics, artwork and high-value possessions receive bespoke packing treatment. Every box is labelled, inventoried and documented for customs purposes.
  • Customs documentation: We prepare and assist with the full customs documentation package required for UAE import. This includes the packing inventory, valued item declarations and any other paperwork specific to your shipment and circumstances.
  • Specialist secure shipping: We offer both sea freight and air freight options depending on your timeline and budget. Sea freight in a dedicated or shared container is the most cost-effective option for full household moves. Air freight is faster but significantly more expensive and is typically used for urgent or smaller shipments.
  • Destination support in Dubai: We coordinate delivery to your Dubai address through our established UAE partner network. Our destination team handles customs clearance at Dubai port, final delivery to your home and, if required, unpacking and placement.

Sea freight vs air freight for Dubai moves

The choice between sea and air freight for a UK to Dubai removal depends on several factors including the volume of your shipment, your timeline, your budget and the nature of the items being shipped.

Sea freight in a dedicated 20-foot or 40-foot container is the standard choice for full household moves. Transit time from a UK port to Dubai port is typically 20 to 28 days, plus customs clearance at the Dubai end which typically adds 5 to 10 working days. The total door-to-door time for a sea freight move is usually 5 to 8 weeks from packing date to delivery at your Dubai address.

Shared container shipping — where your belongings share a container with other customers' items — is a more cost-effective option for smaller volumes. The transit time is similar but the loading and unloading scheduling is more variable.

Air freight is significantly faster — typically 5 to 10 days door to door — but costs substantially more per cubic metre. It is generally only cost-effective for small, high-value or time-critical shipments. We will discuss the most appropriate option for your specific situation during your free consultation.

Prohibited and restricted items for UAE import

The UAE has strict regulations on what can be imported, and it is important to be aware of these before packing. Items that cannot be shipped to Dubai include alcohol, pork products and pork-derived items, narcotics and controlled substances, weapons, ammunition and replica weapons, politically sensitive material, certain medications that are controlled in the UAE, and pornographic material. Some items may be permitted but require special documentation or prior approval from UAE authorities.

We provide a full prohibited and restricted items list as part of our international consultation and will work with you to identify any items that may need alternative arrangements. Our experience with the UAE customs process means we can identify potential issues before they arise rather than discovering them at the point of import.
