London to Dubai: our specialist corridor

Thousands of British families, professionals and businesses relocate to Dubai every year. Relokates has deep expertise on this route: we understand UAE customs requirements, prohibited items, documentation requirements and realistic timelines from years of managing London to Dubai relocations.

What our London to Dubai service includes

  • Pre-move consultation covering customs, documentation, prohibited items and timeline
  • Professional export packing using export-grade double-walled boxes and specialist materials
  • Full customs documentation: packing inventories, valued item declarations and UAE import paperwork
  • Sea freight or air freight depending on your timeline and budget
  • UAE customs clearance managed through our established partner network
  • Final delivery and placement at your Dubai address

Sea freight vs air freight

Sea freight in a dedicated container is the most cost-effective option for full household moves: typically 5 to 9 weeks door to door. Air freight is significantly faster at 5 to 10 days but substantially more expensive. We discuss both options during your free consultation.
