The five reasons people call us last minute

🔗

Chain collapsed

The buyer pulled out, the chain reorganised, and your completion is now 3 days away on a different date. We move chain-collapse customers every week - call with both postcodes and we will quote.

📝

Completion date shifted forward

Solicitor confirmed an earlier completion than you were ready for. Common with cash buyers and new builds. We can absorb 48-hour shifts on most weekday moves.

⚠️

Eviction or notice period ending

Section 21 deadline tomorrow, or a notice period ending sooner than planned. Discretion guaranteed. We have moved tenants with as little as 24 hours notice.

🏢

Urgent corporate relocation

Lease expiry, business sale completion or sudden office reshuffle. We carry out commercial relocations evenings and weekends to minimise downtime.

👀

Your current removal company let you down

Cancelled the day before, increased the price at the last minute, or stopped answering the phone. Call us - we honour the original fixed price agreed on call.

🏠

Property sale completed faster than expected

Quick sale, cash buyer, or auction completion. Common with downsizers and probate sales. We handle these with discretion and a tight timeline.

What "last minute" really means

For us, last minute is any move booked with less than 7 days notice. Here's the realistic availability picture:

Today (same day)

See our same day removals page. Small jobs often possible with 2-4 hours notice. Call immediately.

Tomorrow (24 hours)

High availability for jobs up to 3-bed weekday moves. Saturday/Sunday vans available. Confirmed on call.

Within 48-72 hours

Almost always available including weekends. Time slot flexibility is good. Best window for booking last minute packing alongside.

Within 7 days

Available on almost any day including end-of-month and Fridays (our busiest slots). Multiple time options.

End-of-month Friday

Last Friday of the month is the most heavily booked day in the industry. Call as early in the week as possible - we still have availability but slots go fastest.

Do not pack yourself if you do not have to

Last minute packing is one of the smartest spends in a rushed move. Our crew can pack an average 2-bed property in 4-6 hours, using premium materials that protect against breakages. You stay in control, we do the heavy lifting.

Rushed self-packing is the single most common cause of damaged belongings and missed items. If you have less than 48 hours, ask us to quote packing on the same call as the move.
