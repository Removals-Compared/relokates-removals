When same-day removals usually work

We run roughly one same-day job every working day. The honest factors are how soon you call, how big the move is, and where you're moving from and to. Here's what realistic looks like:

🏠

Single-van man and van

Studio, 1-bed flat, single-room move or single-item delivery. Often available with 2-4 hours notice. Highest same-day success rate.

📦

Two-bed flat moves

Possible if booked before 10am. Subject to van availability and route. Call early in the day for the best chance.

⚡

Emergency completions

Chain collapsed, key release moved up, eviction, urgent corporate relocation - we prioritise these. Always call regardless of size.

What we need from you on the call

Have these ready when you call so we can quote and confirm in one conversation:

  • Both postcodes - pickup and delivery
  • Property type - flat, house, office, number of bedrooms
  • Floor + lift access - third floor walk-up changes the time estimate
  • Approximate volume - rough number of boxes plus large items (sofa, bed, wardrobe, fridge)
  • Earliest time you can be on site - and your deadline for completing the move
  • Any access constraints - parking restrictions, narrow doorways, no lift

Why we don't overcommit on same-day

Other companies say yes to every same-day call and then cancel or arrive late. We have built our reputation on the opposite: if we say we are coming today, we are coming today. The only way we can keep that promise is by being honest when we cannot fit a job in. If we say no on a call, it is because saying yes would mean letting another customer down.
