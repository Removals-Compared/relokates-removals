House removals in City of London

Relokates provides professional, fully insured house removal services throughout City of London and all surrounding areas. Whether you are moving within the borough, relocating to another part of London, or moving out of the capital to Essex, Kent or West Sussex, our team manages every aspect of your move from the first call to the final box in your new home.

The City of London: the Square Mile: is the historic financial heart of the UK and one of the world's most important business districts. While its residential population is tiny compared to other boroughs, the Barbican Estate and Golden Lane Estate provide significant residential communities alongside the commercial towers. Moving in the City requires specialist knowledge of the City's complex access restrictions, loading bay systems, security requirements at many commercial buildings and the extremely limited on-street parking. The City has specific rules for removal vehicles that differ from the rest of London.

Property in City of London

The Barbican is one of London's most iconic residential complexes: a Brutalist estate of apartments, maisonettes and townhouses built in the 1970s. The City has limited period residential housing, with most homes in converted commercial buildings and modern apartment developments.

Whatever the size or type of property you are moving from or into in City of London, Relokates has the vehicles, equipment and experience to manage the move correctly. We carry out a thorough pre-move assessment of access conditions at both your current and new address before providing a fixed-price quote, so that there are no surprises on moving day.

Transport and access in City of London

The City is one of the best-served areas in the UK for public transport: Bank, Monument, Mansion House, Cannon Street, Liverpool Street, Moorgate, Barbican, Aldgate and Fenchurch Street all within the Square Mile.

Understanding the transport geography of City of London informs how we plan every removal in the borough. We know which streets have permit parking zones that require suspension applications, which areas have loading restrictions during certain hours, which roads have restricted access for larger vehicles, and which routes provide the most efficient access during different times of day. This local knowledge makes a real difference on moving day.

Parking suspensions in City of London

Most house removals in City of London require a parking suspension to allow our vehicle to park directly outside your property. Without a suspension, our crew may be forced to park some distance away, turning the move into a long-carry exercise that adds time and difficulty to the job. We apply for parking suspensions on your behalf as part of our standard City of London removal service. We are familiar with the application requirements, lead times and fees of the relevant London borough council and will handle the entire process for you.

We recommend contacting us as early as possible after your exchange of contracts to allow sufficient time for the parking suspension application. Most London borough councils require a minimum of five to ten working days for suspension applications, and popular moving dates can see parking restrictions on particular streets already in place, requiring us to find an alternative solution.
