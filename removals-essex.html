Removal company covering all of Essex

Essex is one of the most diverse counties in the South East: from the buzzing commuter towns of Chelmsford, Brentwood and Billericay along the main London railway corridors to the coastal character of Southend-on-Sea, Leigh-on-Sea and Clacton-on-Sea, from the market town atmosphere of Saffron Walden and Braintree in the north-west to the industrial and port communities of Harwich and Tilbury. Relokates has deep local knowledge of the county and covers 65 towns and locations across Essex as part of our 475-location UK network.

Essex is one of our core operating areas. Many of our moves involve relocations between Essex towns: Chelmsford to Basildon, Southend to Brentwood, Colchester to Harlow: as well as moves from London into Essex and from Essex into Kent or West Sussex. We know the roads, the parking challenges and the specific characteristics of each Essex town we serve.

Key Essex towns we serve

Chelmsford: Essex's county town and a major commuter hub. Fast trains to Liverpool Street in under 30 minutes make it one of the most popular locations for London workers relocating out of the capital. We carry out a large volume of London to Chelmsford moves and are familiar with every area of the city.

Basildon: One of Essex's most populous towns and a designated new town built to rehouse London's post-war population. The town has distinct neighbourhoods including Laindon, Pitsea and Vange, each with different access characteristics.

Colchester: Britain's oldest recorded town and a significant university city. The mix of students, established residents and a growing professional population creates a high volume of removal activity throughout the year.

Southend-on-Sea: Essex's coastal city and one of the most popular seaside destinations in the South East. The high volume of property transactions along the Thames Estuary coast generates a consistent level of removal activity.

Grays: A key town in the Thurrock unitary authority with strong transport links via the A13 and Elizabeth line connections from nearby Grays station.

Brentwood: An affluent commuter town close to the M25 and a popular destination for London families seeking more space. The mix of period properties and newer developments requires a versatile approach to removal planning.
