House removals in Hammersmith and Fulham

Relokates provides professional, fully insured house removal services throughout Hammersmith and Fulham and all surrounding areas. Whether you are moving within the borough, relocating to another part of London, or moving out of the capital to Essex, Kent or West Sussex, our team manages every aspect of your move from the first call to the final box in your new home.

Hammersmith and Fulham has some of west London's most desirable addresses. Fulham and Parsons Green attract a largely affluent professional demographic. Hammersmith itself is a major commercial and transport hub. Shepherd's Bush is home to Westfield London: one of Europe's largest urban shopping centres. The borough sits between the Thames to the south and the elevated Westway to the north, and its streets require careful navigation for removal vehicles particularly around Fulham and the riverside areas.

Whatever the size or type of property you are moving from or into in Hammersmith and Fulham, Relokates has the vehicles, equipment and experience to manage the move correctly. We carry out a thorough pre-move assessment of access conditions at both your current and new address before providing a fixed-price quote, so that there are no surprises on moving day.

Transport and access in Hammersmith and Fulham

District and Piccadilly lines at Hammersmith. District line through Parsons Green and Barons Court. Central line at Shepherd's Bush. Overground at Shepherd's Bush Market and Kensington Olympia.

Understanding the transport geography of Hammersmith and Fulham informs how we plan every removal in the borough. We know which streets have permit parking zones that require suspension applications, which areas have loading restrictions during certain hours, which roads have restricted access for larger vehicles, and which routes provide the most efficient access during different times of day. This local knowledge makes a real difference on moving day.

Parking suspensions in Hammersmith and Fulham

Most house removals in Hammersmith and Fulham require a parking suspension to allow our vehicle to park directly outside your property. Without a suspension, our crew may be forced to park some distance away, turning the move into a long-carry exercise that adds time and difficulty to the job. We apply for parking suspensions on your behalf as part of our standard Hammersmith and Fulham removal service. We are familiar with the application requirements, lead times and fees of the relevant London borough council and will handle the entire process for you.

We recommend contacting us as early as possible after your exchange of contracts to allow sufficient time for the parking suspension application. Most London borough councils require a minimum of five to ten working days for suspension applications, and popular moving dates can see parking restrictions on particular streets already in place, requiring us to find an alternative solution.
