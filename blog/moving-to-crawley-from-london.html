Moving to Crawley from London: A Complete Relocation Guide

Published 8 Jul 2025  |  6 min read  |  By Relokates Removals

Crawley is the most practical commuter option in West Sussex - fast trains to London Victoria and London Bridge throughout the day, affordable property, good schools and the additional benefit of proximity to Gatwick Airport for frequent travellers. A planned post-war new town that has matured significantly, Crawley now offers a genuine community feel across its distinct neighbourhoods.

Commuting from Crawley

Fast trains from Three Bridges - the main railway hub for the Crawley area - to London Bridge take around 35 minutes. Services to London Victoria take a similar time. Annual season tickets cost approximately £3,200 to £3,600 - among the most affordable for the journey time in the South East.

Property in Crawley

Outstanding value for the South East. A four-bedroom detached family home in Pound Hill or Worth costs between £400,000 and £550,000. The town was planned with distinct neighbourhoods each with their own character and schools.

Best areas for London movers

Pound Hill

The most popular neighbourhood for London movers - north of the town centre, close to the M23 and Three Bridges station. Good schools and a range of property types.

Worth and Maidenbower

Newer neighbourhoods in the east of the town with modern housing, good primary schools and easy station access. Popular with young families.

Copthorne and Turners Hill

For those seeking a village feel at Crawley prices - within easy distance of Three Bridges station and offering genuine countryside character.

Gatwick Airport proximity

Crawley sits immediately adjacent to Gatwick Airport - an advantage for frequent flyers but a consideration for those sensitive to aircraft noise. Check the flight path for any specific property before committing.

Book early for Crawley moves

Crawley is a popular destination for London movers and our Friday and weekend slots fill quickly. Book your removal as soon as you have a confirmed completion date.
