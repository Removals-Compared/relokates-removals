Moving to Guildford from London: A Complete Guide

Published 3 Jun 2025  |  7 min read  |  By Relokates Removals

Guildford is one of the most sought-after relocation destinations for London professionals. Surrey's county town sits within the Surrey Hills AONB, offers trains to London Waterloo in as little as 30 minutes, has outstanding schools and provides a quality of life dramatically different from London while remaining genuinely connected to it.

Commuting from Guildford

The fastest trains to London Waterloo take 28 to 34 minutes with services every 15 minutes at peak times. Annual season tickets cost approximately £4,000 to £4,500. Waterloo connects to the Jubilee, Bakerloo and Northern lines, making most of central London accessible within an hour door to door.

Property in Guildford

Guildford commands premium Surrey prices - a detached four-bedroom family home typically costs between £700,000 and £1.2 million. However for families leaving Zone 2 or Zone 3 London, the value comparison in space and quality of environment is still strongly favourable. Popular areas include Merrow and Burpham to the east, Shalford to the south and the surrounding villages of Compton and Godalming.

Schools

The Royal Grammar School is one of England's most academically successful selective state schools. Tormead and Guildford High are leading independent girls' schools. Strong non-selective state options include George Abbot School. The University of Surrey provides a significant academic community.

Guildford lifestyle

The cobbled High Street, independent shops, award-winning restaurants and the Yvonne Arnaud Theatre make Guildford one of the most complete market towns in the South East. The Surrey Hills to the south provide outstanding walking and cycling. Box Hill is 20 minutes away. The North Downs Way runs through the hills above the town.

Surrey lanes can be challenging for large vehicles

Many desirable Surrey properties sit on narrow private lanes or in locations with restricted access. Relokates surveys access at both ends of every move and selects the right vehicle to ensure moving day runs without issues.
