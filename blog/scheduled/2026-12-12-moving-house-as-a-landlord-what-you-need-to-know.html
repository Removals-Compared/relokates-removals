Moving House as a Landlord: What You Need to Know

Published by Relokates Removals · 2026-12-12

Landlords moving home face a different set of practical and financial considerations from owner-occupiers. Tenants, void periods, furniture between properties, capital gains tax and HMRC notifications all add layers of complexity. Here is the practical guide.

Selling a tenanted property

Selling with tenants in situ is possible but limits your buyer pool to other investors. Most buyers want vacant possession. If you need vacant possession to sell at the best price, you will need to serve the appropriate notice to your tenants - a Section 21 notice for an assured shorthold tenancy - and allow the statutory notice period. Factor this timeline into your sale planning.

Furniture and fittings between properties

Landlords frequently have furniture in rental properties that needs to move when a tenancy ends. This might need to go into storage while waiting for a new tenant, into your new home, or to a different rental property. Relokates manages multi-destination moves and can collect from a rental property and deliver to multiple addresses in a single operation.

HMRC notifications

If you are selling a rental property that has increased in value since you purchased it, Capital Gains Tax may be due. The rules around Principal Private Residence Relief and Lettings Relief have changed significantly in recent years. Take specialist tax advice before completing the sale - not after. The timing of your sale and how you structure the transaction can affect your tax liability significantly.

The void period problem

Landlords moving between their own home and a rental property often face a void period where they are paying both a mortgage and rent, or two mortgages simultaneously. Plan the timeline of your sale, purchase and any tenancy ending dates carefully to minimise the overlap period.

House clearance for end-of-tenancy properties

When a long-term tenancy ends and a property needs clearing before sale or re-letting, a professional house clearance service handles everything efficiently.
