Moving House in a Cost of Living Crisis: Cut Costs Without Cutting Corners

Published by Relokates Removals · 2027-05-12

Removal costs, like everything else, have risen. The temptation when budgets are tight is to cut corners on the removal itself - use an uninsured company, do it yourself, reduce the crew size. Here is how to genuinely reduce your removal costs without compromising on the quality and safety of your move.

Move midweek and mid-month

Friday completions and end-of-month moves are the most expensive days to move. Removal companies charge more on their busiest days and have less flexibility. If your completion date is negotiable, ask your solicitor whether a Tuesday or Wednesday in the middle of the month is possible. The saving can be significant and the service is identical.

Declutter aggressively before your survey

Removal costs are based on volume. Every cubic metre you reduce before the survey visit reduces the quoted price. A serious declutter before the survey - not after it - is one of the most effective cost reduction tools available. Sell items on Facebook Marketplace and Ebay in the weeks before the survey and the proceeds offset the removal cost directly.

Pack non-fragile items yourself

A full packing service adds cost. A part-pack service where you pack robust items - books, clothes, bedding, toys - and the professionals pack fragile items - china, glassware, artwork, electronics - costs significantly less than a full pack. You get the protection where it matters while reducing the overall service cost.

Buy packing materials from your removal company

Professional removal companies sell packing materials at trade prices. Double-walled removal boxes from a removal company are cheaper and significantly better quality than boxes from a stationery shop or supermarket. Ask for a materials-only quote and pack yourself with professional materials.

What not to cut

Insurance. Do not use an uninsured removal company. The saving is not worth the risk - one damaged piece of furniture or a broken television can cost more than the entire removal fee. Always ask for insurance documentation before booking.

Crew size. If your removal company has assessed your property and recommended three people, using two to save money will result in a longer job and potentially additional time charges. Trust the assessment.
