Moving from London to West Sussex: A Complete Guide

Published 13 May 2025  |  7 min read  |  By Relokates Removals

West Sussex offers London movers a compelling combination of coastal access, outstanding countryside, excellent schools and dramatically better value than equivalent London properties. From the vibrant city of Brighton and Hove to the market towns of Horsham and Arundel, the county has something for almost every lifestyle and budget.

Why Londoners choose West Sussex

The financial case is strong. A budget of £600,000 that buys a two-bedroom flat in most London zones buys a four or five-bedroom detached house in many parts of West Sussex. The quality of life difference is equally significant - beaches, the South Downs National Park, an improving food and drink scene and genuine community feel are the most common reasons given by London leavers for their choice of West Sussex.

Best towns for London movers

Crawley

The most practical commuter option in West Sussex, Crawley offers fast trains to London Victoria and London Bridge in around 35 minutes. Property prices are significantly lower than surrounding areas and the town has good schools and amenities. Popular with young families leaving South London.

Horsham

A prosperous market town at the heart of the county, Horsham has direct trains to London Victoria in around 55 minutes, excellent schools including a well-regarded grammar and a vibrant independent town centre. Consistently rated among the best places to live in the South East.

Haywards Heath

One of the strongest commuter towns in the county, Haywards Heath offers fast trains to London Bridge and Victoria in around 45 minutes. Popular with professionals who want good London access without premium prices.

Chichester

A cathedral city with world-class cultural institutions including the Festival Theatre, Chichester is popular with those less dependent on daily London commuting. Trains to London Victoria take around 90 minutes. The harbour, the Downs and the coastal plain make the setting exceptional.

Worthing

A coastal town with a growing reputation for food, creativity and coastal living, Worthing has attracted a younger demographic of Londoners seeking the sea at affordable prices. Trains to London Victoria take around 70 minutes.

What to expect on moving day

A London to West Sussex move typically takes between five and ten hours depending on the volume and specific locations. Coastal destinations involve a longer drive - plan accordingly. Relokates advises on the best routes and timing for every move to avoid motorway congestion on the A23 and A24.
