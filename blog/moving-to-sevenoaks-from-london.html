Moving to Sevenoaks from London: Everything You Need to Know

Published 24 Jun 2025  |  7 min read  |  By Relokates Removals

Sevenoaks is one of the most desirable relocation destinations in the South East - a market town set within the Kent Downs AONB with trains to London Cannon Street in as little as 22 minutes and some of the finest schools in England. For London professionals seeking the quintessential English countryside lifestyle within practical commuting distance of the City, Sevenoaks is the benchmark.

Commuting from Sevenoaks

The fastest trains to London Cannon Street and Charing Cross take between 22 and 30 minutes with frequent services throughout the day. Annual season tickets cost approximately £3,800 to £4,200 - excellent value for the speed. Sevenoaks is one of the most efficient commuter propositions in the South East.

Property in Sevenoaks

Sevenoaks commands premium Kent prices - a detached four-bedroom family home costs between £700,000 and £1.5 million. For families leaving Zone 1 or Zone 2 London, the comparison in space, schools and living environment is still strongly favourable. Surrounding villages - Otford, Eynsford, Shoreham, Westerham - offer slightly lower prices with similar access.

Schools

Sevenoaks School is one of England's finest independent schools. Knole Academy is a well-regarded comprehensive. The Kent grammar school system provides additional selective options at 11+ in nearby Tonbridge. The educational offer is exceptional at every level.

The Sevenoaks lifestyle

Knole Park - one of England's largest deer parks, home to the National Trust's Knole House - is on the edge of the town centre. An independent high street, good restaurants and extraordinary countryside access via the North Downs Way and the Kent Weald make Sevenoaks one of the most complete market town environments in England.

Many Sevenoaks properties require careful access planning

Private lanes, gated developments and restricted locations are common in the Sevenoaks area. Relokates surveys access at both ends of every move and selects the right vehicle to ensure moving day runs without issues.
