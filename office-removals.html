Office removals that keep your business moving

A poorly managed office relocation can cost a business far more than the removal fee itself. At Relokates, we understand that your business cannot afford unnecessary disruption, and we plan every commercial relocation accordingly: from pre-move survey through to the moment your team walks into a fully functional new office.

Out-of-hours office moves

The majority of our commercial clients choose to move outside business hours. We offer evening, overnight and weekend moves across all our coverage areas: so your team walks into a fully operational new office on Monday morning without a single day of lost productivity.

What our office removal service covers

  • Pre-move survey and planning at both premises before the move date
  • IT equipment handling: computers, monitors, servers and AV systems
  • Server room relocations coordinated with your IT team
  • Furniture and workstations disassembled and reassembled
  • Labelling and inventory system so every item arrives in the right place
  • Archive and document handling with full discretion
