IT and technology - the most critical part of any office move

IT infrastructure is almost always the critical path in any office relocation. Before anything else is decided, your IT team or managed IT provider must be involved in the planning. Key questions to resolve before moving day:

Broadband installation lead time

Commercial broadband at a new office can take 4 to 8 weeks from order to activation. Book immediately on signing the lease - not two weeks before moving day.

Server migration or cloud transition

If your servers are being physically moved, coordinate the shutdown sequence with your IT provider. If you are migrating to cloud infrastructure, the office move is the ideal time to make the transition.

Phone systems and VoIP

VoIP systems require active broadband at the new address to function. Temporary SIM-based solutions are available to bridge the gap if broadband is delayed.
