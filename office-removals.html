Office removals that keep your business moving

A poorly managed office relocation can cost a business far more than the removal fee itself. Lost productivity, damaged equipment, confused staff and clients unable to reach you during the transition — the real cost of a badly executed office move can run into thousands of pounds in lost business alone. At Relokates, we understand that your business cannot afford unnecessary disruption, and we plan every commercial relocation accordingly.

Our office removal service covers businesses of every size across London, Essex, Kent and West Sussex — from single-person professional offices moving from a serviced workspace to purpose-built premises, to multi-floor corporate relocations requiring careful coordination across multiple departments. We have managed moves for professional services firms, retail businesses, warehouses, medical practices, educational institutions and technology companies. Every move is planned with the same rigour and executed with the same care.

Out-of-hours office moves

The majority of our commercial clients choose to move outside business hours to minimise disruption to their operations and their clients. Relokates offers out-of-hours office removals including evening moves, overnight moves and weekend moves across all our coverage areas.

An overnight office move works as follows: our crew arrives at your current premises after business closes, typically between 6pm and 8pm. We systematically pack, label and move every item from your current location. By the time your team arrives at the new office on Monday morning, everything is in place, connected and ready to go. When a move is planned and executed properly, your staff should not be able to tell that anything happened over the weekend except that they are now in a different building.

What our office removal service covers

  • Pre-move survey and planning: We visit your current and new premises before the move date to assess access, plan the layout of the new office and identify any logistical challenges. This planning phase is what separates a smooth office move from a chaotic one.
  • IT equipment handling: Computers, monitors, servers, printers, networking equipment, AV systems and telecommunications infrastructure all require careful handling. Our crew is trained in the safe disconnection, packing, transport and reconnection of office IT equipment.
  • Server room and data centre moves: Server room relocations require the highest level of care and planning. We work with your IT team to coordinate a safe shutdown and transport of server hardware, ensuring that downtime is minimised and that equipment is handled according to manufacturer specifications.
  • Furniture and workstation moves: Desks, chairs, filing cabinets, storage units, meeting room furniture, reception counters and specialist equipment are all moved, disassembled where necessary and reassembled in the correct position at your new premises.
  • Archive and document handling: Confidential documents and archives are handled securely. We can arrange for sealed boxes to be transported and stored in secure facilities if required during an office transition period.
  • Labelling and inventory management: For larger office moves, we provide a labelling system that ensures every box, piece of equipment and item of furniture is assigned to the correct department or workstation at the new location. This eliminates the confusion that often follows large office moves when staff cannot find their equipment or documents.

Office removals across London

London is home to the vast majority of our commercial clients. We carry out office removals across all central London areas including the City of London, Canary Wharf, Westminster, Southwark, Holborn, Clerkenwell, Shoreditch and all other commercial districts. We also serve businesses in outer London boroughs including Bromley, Croydon, Bexley, Havering, Redbridge and all 33 boroughs.

London office moves present specific challenges including restricted parking in commercial zones, building access restrictions, loading bay scheduling with property management companies, and lift access in high-rise office buildings. We have extensive experience navigating all of these challenges and have established working relationships with property managers at many of London's major commercial buildings.

Office removals across Essex, Kent and West Sussex

Outside London, we serve businesses across the commuter belt and beyond. Essex has a strong business community centred on towns such as Chelmsford, Colchester, Basildon, Harlow and Brentwood, and we carry out regular commercial moves across the county. Kent's business base ranges from logistics and distribution companies near the M25 and M20 to professional services firms in Maidstone, Canterbury and the Medway towns. West Sussex has a growing business community in Crawley, Horsham and Chichester, and we serve businesses across the county.

How we price office removals

Office removal quotes are based on the volume of items to be moved, the distance between the two premises, access conditions at both locations, whether the move is to be carried out during or outside business hours, and the specific requirements of the move including IT handling, server room moves or archive management. All quotes are fixed price — the figure we agree is the figure you are invoiced.

We do not provide ballpark estimates for commercial moves. We carry out a proper assessment of your requirements before providing a quote so that the figure is accurate and binding.
