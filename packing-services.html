Why professional packing makes the difference

Most people underestimate how long it takes to pack a property properly: and how much difference the right technique and materials make. A three-bedroom house takes an experienced packer around 6 to 8 hours to pack safely. Our professional packers use the correct materials, correct techniques and arrive prepared so that every item that leaves your property is properly protected for transit.

Our packing service options

  • Full pack: Our team packs your entire home: every room, every item: using professional materials. The most comprehensive option, recommended for larger moves and properties with fragile or valuable items.
  • Part pack: We pack the items you feel less confident packing yourself: typically breakables, glassware, china, artwork, mirrors and electronics. You pack the straightforward items.
  • Materials only: We supply professional-grade packing materials for you to use yourself: significantly better quality than supermarket boxes.

The materials we use

  • Double-walled boxes: far stronger than standard supermarket boxes
  • Bubble wrap: commercial-grade for fragile items and electronics
  • Packing paper: acid-free to wrap individual items
  • Wardrobe boxes: with integrated hanging rail for clothes
  • Mirror and picture boxes: telescopic, specialist protection
  • Mattress bags: heavy-duty covers for all mattress sizes
