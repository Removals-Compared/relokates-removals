Why professional packing makes the difference

Most people underestimate how long it takes to pack a property properly. A three-bedroom house typically takes an experienced packer around 6 to 8 hours to pack thoroughly and safely. For someone doing it themselves, around a moving day, without specialist materials and without the techniques that professional packers use, the same job takes considerably longer — and the results are often considerably less safe for the items inside the boxes.

Professional packing is not a luxury service reserved for large moves or high-value items. It is a practical investment in the safety of your belongings and the smoothness of your moving day. When you arrive at your new home and every box is correctly labelled, properly packed and intact, the time and cost of professional packing feels very well spent.

Our packing service options

We offer three levels of packing service to suit different needs and budgets:

  • Full pack: Our team arrives at your property — typically the day before your move or on moving day morning — and packs the entire contents of your home into boxes, bags and specialist containers. Every item is wrapped appropriately, labelled clearly with its destination room and packed securely. This is the most comprehensive option and the one we recommend for larger moves, properties with a lot of fragile or valuable items, or customers who simply do not have the time to pack themselves.
  • Part pack: We pack the items you feel less confident packing yourself — typically breakables, glassware, china, artwork, mirrors, electronics, and any items of particular value or fragility. You pack the straightforward items yourself and we handle the specialist packing. This is a popular middle ground that provides professional handling where it matters most while keeping costs lower than a full pack.
  • Materials only: We supply you with a full set of professional packing materials — double-walled boxes in multiple sizes, bubble wrap, packing paper, tape, wardrobe boxes, mirror boxes and specialist covers — and you do the packing yourself. Our materials are significantly better quality than what you will find in a supermarket or DIY store, and we will advise you on how to use them effectively.

The materials we use

The quality of packing materials makes a significant difference to the safety of your belongings during a move. We use professional-grade materials throughout:

  • Double-walled boxes: Standard supermarket boxes are single-walled and collapse under weight or when stacked. Our double-walled boxes are significantly stronger and provide proper protection during loading, transit and unloading.
  • Bubble wrap: Used for all fragile items, glassware, ornaments, electronics and anything breakable. We use proper commercial-grade bubble wrap, not the thin domestic variety.
  • Packing paper: Acid-free packing paper used to wrap individual items within boxes and to fill void space to prevent movement during transit.
  • Wardrobe boxes: Tall boxes with an integrated hanging rail that allow your hanging clothes to be transported still on their hangers, arriving at your new home wrinkle-free and ready to hang.
  • Mirror and picture boxes: Telescopic boxes designed specifically for mirrors, framed pictures and artwork up to a specific size.
  • Mattress bags: Heavy-duty plastic covers that protect mattresses from marks, dirt and damage during the move.
  • Furniture blankets and protective covers: Heavy moving blankets used to wrap larger furniture items for protection during transit.

Packing for international moves

International removals — particularly our specialist London to Dubai service — require a higher standard of packing than domestic moves. Items will be in transit for weeks rather than hours, and the journey involves handling by multiple parties including shipping agents and port handlers. For international moves, we use export-grade packaging throughout, with additional reinforcement for fragile items and bespoke crating for particularly high-value or delicate pieces.

Unpacking services

We also offer a professional unpacking service at your new property. Our team will unpack boxes, remove packing materials and place items where you want them. Combined with our placement service for furniture, this means you can arrive at your new home and find it genuinely ready to live in rather than facing a sea of boxes.
